Privacy Policy

This extension analyzes chess games locally in the user's browser from PGN text provided by the user.

Data Usage

The extension processes chess game data (board positions and moves) locally to provide analysis and feedback.

No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted to external servers.

Third Parties

This extension does not sell, share, or transfer user data to any third parties.

Storage

All processing occurs locally in the browser. No chess data is transmitted to third-party websites or external servers.
